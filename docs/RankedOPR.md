# **[DRAFT]** Ranked OPR Community Project
[![ttv/Goatfryed Status](https://img.shields.io/twitch/status/goatfryed)
](https://www.twitch.tv/goatfryed)

OPRs between 22:00 - 01:30 CEST on nysa will be considered ranked. Match making rantings will be calculated for all players in recorded matches.

Matches are recorded, if supporting streamers joined the match. See [Contributing](#contributing).

This is an experimental community project. Details might change over time. We improve as we go.

## Goals

- Huge shoutout to every active and casual pvp player left
- Additional player motivation
- Fun through competition
- Stat nerding
- You better step point.

### Non-Goals

Shared insights are *not* to be used to insult, flame, mock, harass or otherwise degrade other players on whatever skill level, bottom and top.

## How it works

### Publishing

MMRs are published Fridays, 21:30 CEST on [ttv/goatfryed](https://www.twitch.tv/goatfryed).

We will look together at the top players, the honorable grinders, selected community interests and at everyone who wants to know and share their MMR.

No flaming or shaming. Requests for other people's MMRs might be denied at the organizers discretion.

## Recording

Any twitch clips of a scroll down the scoreboard is viable source for match data.

Supporting streamers will clip and submit the games.

If you are streaming or watching a stream, you can contribute through clipping. Share the clip with game date on [ttv/goatfryed](https://www.twitch.tv/goatfryed). (Process might be subject to change).
